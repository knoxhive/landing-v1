Tokenomics

The term is formed by pairing up the two words, token and economics. So tokenomics basically points to the economics of the crypto token, KNX. It refers to all the qualities of the KNX token that makes it appealing to investors. This should help you grasp the functionality, objective, allocation policy, and more of the KNX token.
